Brie Cranberry Puffs

Thsese wonderfull puff pastry parcels are perfect for perking up your party! A pleasing mix of savoury and sweet, sure to tantilise your tastebuds... And you only need three ingredients.

These are perfect as an apetiser for a sit-down dinner, or as finger food at a party.

Ingredients

  • Puff Pastry (store bought. I like Jus-Roll but use whatever you have available.
  • Cranberry Sauce (Store bought again. I prefer Colmans but again, get whatever is available.
  • Brie - one wheel.
  • Oil or butter.

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 180°.
  2. Uisng a paper towel, grease a mufin tin. If you don't have a mufin tin, line a baking sheet with greaseproof paper.
  3. Roll out your pastry and cut it into squares just larger than the muffin tin holes.
  4. Slice your brie into chunks that will fill the bottom of the holes, and place one chunk into each hole.
  5. Spoon on a teaspoon of cranberry sauce.
  6. Pinch the corners of the overhanging pastry together.
  7. (Optional) Using a pastry brush to brush oil or butter on the exposed pastry to give it a glossy finish.
  8. Sprinkle over some salt. Flaky salt like sea salt is best here, but use what you have.
  9. Bake at 180° for 15-20 minutes or until golden and crispy.
  10. Cool on a wire rack for at least 3 mniutes before eating. Can be served warm or cold.
